JOURNÉE DES DOCTORANTS EEATS

The JDD 2025 will give you an opportunity to meet other PhD students from different laboratories and specialties, to exchange about your doctoral studies experience and to attend round tables and conferences related to hot research topics of EEATS domains. You are also invited to present your thesis work through a flash presentation (of 1 minute) along with a poster. The poster and the presentation can be done either in English (preferably) or in French.
Deadline for registration: 27/04/2025

A big thank you to the doctoral school!
